WordPress Users Workshop

About WordPress Users workshops

WordPress is the world’s most popular website builder, with a quarter of all websites on the internet today built in WordPress. Our workshop is suitable for anyone who wants to learn more about updating, improving and maintaining your existing WordPress website.

What you will learn

  • Creating and formatting your posts and pages
  • Inserting and optimising media – images, video
  • How to make sure your website is optimised for Search Engines (SEO)
  • Integrating your content with social media
  • Comment moderation
  • Using and modifying themes
  • Using plugins and widgets to improve your website’s functionality
  • Managing multiple WordPress Users
  • Website Security

How the day runs

Arrive from 9:15am, while we don't start until 9.30am, there is tea/coffee and pastries to kick start your day. We break for an hour at 1pm and we aim to finish at 4.30pm.

You will need to bring a laptop (with wi-fi) as the course is ‘hands-on’. No special software is needed for the day except a modern web browser installed (Our preference is for the latest version of Google Chrome or Firefox).

Workshops are limited to 6-8 attendees.

Location

82a South Circular Road, Dublin 8, Ireland

Workshops are run in a bright and quiet space above The Little Bird Cafe on South Circular Road, just a 10 minute walk from Camden Street. Entrance is to the right of the cafe, the first door on Spencer Street.

Your WordPress Trainer

Cathy Scanlon (B.Eng. PhD.), is a professional Web Development Specialist and holds a postgraduate qualification in Teaching and Learning. Cathy previously held a Lecturing position at National University of Ireland, Galway and receives fantastic feedback from workshop attendee.
Cathy was the Digital Director at a London based Design Agency for a number of years, creating websites for a range of clients from large multinational banks, retail and charities.
