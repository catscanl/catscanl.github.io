Social Media for Business Workshop

About social media workshops

Social media platforms enable businesses to reach wide audiences quickly and effectively. Our workshop is ideal for those new to using social media for generating business leads.

What you will learn

  • What is social media for business?
  • Overview of social media platforms; when to use what?
  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • LinkedIn
  • YouTube and Video Marketing
  • Blogging
  • Pinterest & Instagram
  • Managing your social presence

How the day runs

Arrive from 9:15am, while we don't start until 9.30am, there is tea/coffee and pastries to kick start your day. We break for an hour at 1pm and we aim to finish at 4.30pm.

You will need to bring a laptop (with wi-fi) as the course is ‘hands-on’. No special software is needed for the day except a modern web browser installed (Our preference is for the latest version of Google Chrome or Firefox).

Workshops are limited to 6-8 attendees.

Location

82a South Circular Road, Dublin 8, Ireland

Workshops are run in a bright and quiet space above The Little Bird Cafe on South Circular Road, just a 10 minute walk from Camden Street. Entrance is to the right of the cafe, the first door on Spencer Street.
